About ASHV

ASHV 2K26 is a grand MITS university fest that celebrates talent, innovation, culture, and competitive spirit. Inspired by the Sanskrit word “Ashv” (Horse), the event symbolizes speed, strength, determination, and victory. It provides a dynamic platform for students to showcase their technical expertise, cultural creativity, and leadership skills. ASHV (अश्व) in Sanskrit means Horse — symbol of power, speed and victory.
ASHV 2K26 is a grand fusion of technical brilliance, cultural energy and competitive spirit.
ASHV represents power, momentum, and excellence A fusion of technology, culture, sports, and creativity
Encouraging students to compete, collaborate, and innovate

Objectives of ASHV 2K26

To nurture technical and creative talents among students
To promote innovation, leadership, and collaboration
To provide exposure beyond academics
To encourage healthy competition and sportsmanship

🏆 Event Categories

Technical Events – Coding, Hackathons, Innovation challenges
Cultural Events – Dance, Music, Drama, Arts
Sports Events – Indoor and Outdoor competitions
Gaming Events – Competitive e-sports and fun gaming battles
